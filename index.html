Currently, I am a versatile software engineer, passionate about cloud computing, DevOps, and process automation. I combine my skills in programming, problem-solving, and designing complex architectures to develop innovative and efficient solutions.

With strong creativity, excellent organization, and a high level of autonomy, I am always motivated to learn, share my knowledge, and tackle new challenges. My meticulous organization is a key asset in achieving goals while adapting my actions to constraints.
